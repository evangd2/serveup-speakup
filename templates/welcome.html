Welcome to Serve Up, Speak Up

Serve Up, Speak Up is a resource for people who want to become more involved in activism and social justice, but don't know how or where to start.

Go to the Volunteer page to find volunteer and service organizations as well as opportunities in your area.

In the Representatives page, enter your address and you'll find your local Congress representatives, their contact information, and their social media accounts. Along with this, there are fill-in-the-blank templates for you to use on the Representatives page if you want to send a letter, email, or call your representative, but don't know what to say.

Check out the News page to find the latest news articles in categories like activism, politics, the environment, education, and social justice.

Want to know about activism events, marches, and protests? Go to the Events page, where you'll find a calendar listing events near you once you enter an address. You'll also find the latest tweets from prominent activists so you can stay informed.

Feel free to email the team behind Serve Up, Speak Up at serveup.speakup@gmail.com if you have any questions, comments, or concerns.
